## Required structure

1. `source/` for untouched original assets.
2. `master/` for approved transparent and vector masters.
3. `exports/linkedin/` for Page, Group, and newsletter assets.
4. `exports/web/` for website, favicon, and social preview assets.
5. `exports/print/` for print quality files.
6. `templates/` for approved social and marketing templates.
