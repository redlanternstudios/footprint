## OFFICIAL DESIGN

The official Footprint logo is the vertically arranged black mark selected by Ro on 2026 07 13.

It contains:

1. A hand rendered human footprint symbol.
2. A lowercase hand rendered `footprint` wordmark.
3. A mandatory terminal period after the wordmark.
4. A centered vertical relationship between symbol and wordmark.

The irregular ink texture is intentional and part of the identity. It communicates human presence, imperfect real work, and movement rather than polished corporate uniformity.

## BRAND MEANING

The human footprint represents evidence that someone was here, made something, tried something, or moved something forward.

The handmade treatment supports the Footprint promise to notice real work before it becomes perfectly packaged.

The terminal period gives the name confidence and finality. It is part of the official wordmark and may not be removed.

## CLEAR SPACE

Until the vector master is complete, preserve clear space equal to at least the width of the smallest toe mark on every side of the full lockup.

Final clear space and minimum size measurements require the production vector and small size tests.

## PROHIBITED USE

1. Do not remove the terminal period.
2. Do not recreate the wordmark with a standard font.
3. Do not smooth away the hand rendered ink texture.
4. Do not stretch or compress the mark.
5. Do not rotate the footprint.
6. Do not add gradients, shadows, outlines, bevels, or glow.
7. Do not place the mark on a busy image without sufficient contrast.
8. Do not publish the checkerboard background.
9. Do not add RedLantern red directly to the mark without approval.
10. Do not use a generic replacement footprint icon.

## ACCESSIBILITY

1. Test the mark on light and dark surfaces.
2. Use the black mark on Paper or another sufficiently light surface.
3. Use the approved white reverse mark on dark surfaces.
4. Pair symbol only uses with accessible text naming Footprint.
5. Do not rely on the symbol alone when the audience may not recognize the brand.
