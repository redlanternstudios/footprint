# Footprint Ownership Architecture

Version: 1.0

Decision date: 2026 07 13

Decision authority: Ro

Status: LOCKED

## OBJECTIVE

Establish one ownership chain for every Footprint domain, account, asset, contract, data store, and public representation.

## CANONICAL HIERARCHY

1. By Red, LLC

Legal owner and contracting entity.

2. RedLantern Studios

Operating studio and builder.

3. Footprint

Independent public community and product brand.

## OWNERSHIP RULES

VERIFIED: By Red, LLC owns Footprint intellectual property, domains, vendor relationships, contracts, data, repositories, and public brand assets.

VERIFIED: RedLantern Studios designs, builds, operates, and stewards Footprint on behalf of By Red, LLC.

VERIFIED: Footprint presents publicly as its own community brand.

VERIFIED: Rory is the founding steward and authorized representative designated by the owner.

PARTIAL: Keymon may be presented as a founding steward only after his written identity and publishing authority record becomes active.

UNKNOWN: Whether RedLantern Studios or Footprint is registered as an assumed business name. No document may use formal assumed name language until registration is verified.

## ACCOUNT OWNERSHIP

Every production account must record:

1. Legal owner: By Red, LLC.
2. Operating team: RedLantern Studios.
3. Project or public brand: Footprint.
4. Primary administrator.
5. Secondary administrator.
6. Billing owner.
7. Recovery method.
8. Two factor authentication status.
9. Renewal date.
10. Last access review.

No production account may be owned solely by Footprint as an unregistered identity or by one personal email address.

## CONTRACT AND VENDOR RULES

1. Contracts use By Red, LLC as the contracting party.
2. Vendor billing uses By Red, LLC.
3. Footprint may appear as the project, product, or account label.
4. RedLantern Studios may appear as the operating team.
5. Formal assumed name language is prohibited until registration is verified.
6. Privacy notices identify By Red, LLC as the data controller or business where legally appropriate and counsel approved.
7. Contributor permissions grant the required rights to By Red, LLC for use through Footprint and its controlled channels.

## ASSET OWNERSHIP

By Red, LLC owns or controls:

1. Footprint name rights after clearance.
2. Domains.
3. Social and professional accounts.
4. Logos and visual identity.
5. Website and application code.
6. Repository and documentation.
7. Workflow definitions.
8. Data model and operational records.
9. Original editorial assets created by the company.
10. Licenses granted by contributors.

Contributors retain ownership of their original work unless a separate written agreement states otherwise. Footprint receives only the rights explicitly granted through the consent record.

## ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

AC1: Every production account identifies By Red, LLC as owner.

AC2: Every public surface identifies Footprint as the public brand.

AC3: Every operating record identifies RedLantern Studios as builder or operator.

AC4: Every privileged account has a primary and secondary administrator.

AC5: Every contract and vendor relationship uses the legal owner unless counsel approves another structure.

AC6: Contributor work ownership remains with the contributor except for explicitly granted rights.

AC7: No public document uses formal assumed name language without verified registration.

## DEFINITION OF DONE

The hierarchy is reflected in the domain registration, account registry, vendor billing, contracts, privacy documents, contributor consent, repository metadata, website footer, LinkedIn About section, and brand guidelines.
